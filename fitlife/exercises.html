Exercise Routines

Choose from a variety of personalized workout routines to help you achieve your fitness goals.


  • Strength Training: Build muscle with bodyweight or equipment-based exercises.
  • Cardio: Boost endurance with running, cycling, and HIIT workouts.
  • Flexibility & Mobility: Improve flexibility with yoga and stretching routines.

Common Exercises

Upper Body (Chest, Back, Arms)

  • Bench Presses - Pressing motion using Dumbbells, good for the chest.
  • Dumbbell Flys - A chest exercise, performed by doing a "fly" motion.
  • Rows - A back exercise, performed by pulling a dumbbell from a bent-over position to your side.
  • Bicep Curls - A bicep exercise, performed by flexing the elbow to lift a dumbbell towards the shoulder.
  • Tricep Dips - A tricep exercise, performed by lowering and raising your body by bending your elbows, targeting the triceps.
  • Shoulder Press - A shoulder exercise, performed by lifting dumbbells or a barbell overhead from shoulder height.

Lower Body (Legs)

  • Squats - A thigh exercise, performed by performing a forward squat motion.
  • Lunges - A leg exercise, performed by performing a lunge motion.
  • Deadlifts - A lower body exercise, performed by lifting a dumbbell or barbell straight down from the ground.
  • Calf Raises - A calf exercise, performed by raising the metatarsals while holding weight.

Common Exercise Routines


  • "Push, Pull, Legs" Routine - A routine that focuses on 3 groups per day for the week: Pushing Motion (Chest and Triceps), Pulling Motion (Back, Biceeps), and Legs as a whole. Can be performed for 3 or 6 days depending on rest time.
  • Upper and Lower Split - A routine that requires you to perform either upper or lower body exercises depending on the day. typically done on the 1st (Upper), 2nd (Lower), 4th (Upper) and 5th (Lower) day of the week.
  • Full Body Workout - A routine that includes all 10 major muscle groups, focusing on 3 groups per day for the week. Can be performed for 3 or 6 days depending on rest time.
  • Custom Workout Split - A routine that is typically given to you by a fitness trainer after consultation or meeting, specifically caters to your goals and limits.
